Easy to Use

Get started in seconds

1. Include the CSS

<!DOCTYPE html> <html data-color-theme="luxury"> <head> <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/ YOUR-USERNAME/solanke-themes@v1.0.0/ solanke-themes.css"> </head> <body data-theme-enabled> <!-- Your content --> </body> </html>

2. Use the Classes

<!-- Buttons --> <button class="btn-theme-primary"> Primary </button> <!-- Cards --> <div class="card-theme hover-lift"> <h3 class="text-gradient-theme"> Card Title </h3> <p class="text-theme-dark"> Content here </p> </div> <!-- Glass Effect --> <div class="glass-theme"> Liquid glass design </div>
